Sliders

Overview

Gameplay

Manipulate and toggle gravity! A round player object has to be raced through a chain of levels, while avoiding all kinds of danger. A tap and hold onto the left half of the screen stops the downwards pull of gravitation. When the tap breaks up, gravity gets activated again, the balls velocity however also gets reset, stealing a lot of its downwards potential. A tap onto the right half of the screen switches the velocity orientations, making the ball for instance fall in a curve to the right instead of a curve to the left.

Rules

Finish a level to unlock the next one. Earn medals by beating the level within time. Medals unlock new usable items in the level editor. Avoid everything that is not round. Collect everything that is round.

Features

50 Free levels, easy to Learn, hard to master Difficulty and enemy appearances rise in each next level Inbuilt Level Editing Distribute your own levels and play community content
